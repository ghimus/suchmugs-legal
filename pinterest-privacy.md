# Suchmugs Pinterest Publisher - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

## What this application is

Suchmugs Pinterest Publisher is a private, first-party tool operated by Suchmugs SRL to
schedule and publish Pins to Pinterest business accounts that Suchmugs owns. It is not a
consumer product and is not offered to the public. No third party can sign in to it.

## Data we access through the Pinterest API

With the account owner's OAuth authorization, the application accesses only:

- The owner's own Pinterest business account profile and board list (to choose a destination board).
- The ability to create Pins on those owned accounts (image, title, description, destination link, alt text).
- Read-only analytics for those owned accounts (impressions, saves, outbound clicks), used to pace posting.

The application does not access, request, or store data about any other Pinterest user, any
follower, or any audience member. It does not read other users' boards, Pins, or messages.

## Data we store

The application stores, on infrastructure controlled by Suchmugs:

- OAuth access and refresh tokens for the owned accounts, used solely to publish on the owner's behalf.
- A local queue of Pins to be published (image references, copy, destination links, schedule times).
- A log of published Pins and their resulting Pinterest Pin IDs and basic performance metrics.

Tokens are stored with restricted access and are never shared with any third party. The
application performs no advertising, no profiling, and no sale of data.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data accessed through the Pinterest API with third parties.
Data is used only to operate the owner's own Pinterest publishing.

## Retention and deletion

Tokens and queue data are retained only while the tool is in use. The account owner can revoke
the application's access at any time from Pinterest account settings, after which the tokens
become invalid and are deleted from our store. Published-Pin logs are retained for performance
review and can be deleted on request.
